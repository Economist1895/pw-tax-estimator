Spouse Relief
$0
▼
ℹ️
You can claim if in the preceding year:
  • Your spouse was living with and supported by you;
  • Your spouse's annual income did not exceed $8,000 (this does not apply if your spouse is physically disabled or mentally impaired); and
  • No other person is claiming Parent Relief, Parent Relief (Disability) or Sibling Relief (Disability) on your spouse.
👶
Qualifying Child Relief (QCR) / Child Relief (Disability)
$0
▼
ℹ️
You can claim this relief if in the preceding year:
  • Your child is unmarried and was/is born to you and your spouse/ex-spouse, a step-child, or legally adopted;
  • Your child was below 16 years old, or 16 and above studying full-time at any educational institution;
  • Your child did not have annual income exceeding $8,000 (this does not apply if your child is physically disabled or mentally impaired); and
  • You have been maintaining your child.
Amount: $4,000 per qualifying child (QCR); $7,500 per child with disability. Both parents combined cannot exceed the per-child maximum — enter only your agreed share.

Parent Relief / Parent Relief (Disability)
$0
▼
ℹ️
You can claim this tax relief if in the preceding year you supported a dependant (e.g. parent, parent-in-law, grandparent, grandparent-in-law) who:
  • Was 55 years of age or above;
  • Did not have annual income of more than $8,000 (does not apply if physically disabled or mentally impaired); and
  • Was living with you in the same household, or in a separate household where you incurred $2,000 or more in support.
No other person may claim Spouse Relief, Spouse Relief (Disability) or Sibling Relief (Disability) on the same dependant. You may claim up to 2 dependants.

NSman Relief (Self / Wife / Parent)
$0
▼
ℹ️
To qualify for NSman Self Relief:
  1. You must have completed your full-time National Service under the Enlistment Act (Cap. 93); or
  2. You are deemed to have completed such service by the proper authority.

Regulars from MINDEF, the Singapore Police Force, the Singapore Civil Defence Force, the Central Narcotics Bureau, the Singapore Prison Service, and NSmen who have committed any disciplinary or criminal offences in the preceding work year are excluded.

Amount of relief
The amount depends on whether you: (1) performed NS activities in the preceding work year*; and (2) are a NS key command and staff appointment holder (KAH).

NS activities performed?General populationKAH
No$1,500$3,500
Yes$3,000$5,000

*For YA 2026, the relevant work year is 1 Apr 2025 to 31 Mar 2026.

Ex-NSmen or NS-liable ex-regular servicemen above the statutory age are given the base quantum of $1,500.
ℹ️
To qualify for NSman Wife Relief ($750):
  1. You are a Singapore Citizen in the preceding year; and
  2. Your husband is eligible for NSman Self Relief.

Widows of deceased NSmen may also be eligible for this relief unless they have re-married.
ℹ️
To qualify for NSman Parent Relief ($750):
  1. You are a Singapore Citizen in the preceding year;
  2. Your child is eligible for NSman Self Relief; and
  3. Your child was born to you and your spouse/ex-spouse, or is your step-child or legally adopted child.

Parents of deceased NSmen are also eligible. If you and your son are both NSmen, you will be eligible for either NSman Self Relief or NSman Parent Relief — whichever is higher.
